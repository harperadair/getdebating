Helpful to know

What to include in your enquiry

To help us reply with something useful, it's helpful to include:

  • Your school or group's name
  • The age or year group of the students involved
  • Roughly how many students would take part
  • What you're looking for — a single workshop, ongoing coaching, or something else

Don't worry if you don't have all of this yet — a rough idea is enough to get started.
