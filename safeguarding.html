Trust & safety

Safeguarding & child protection.

Every school we work with is trusting us with their students. Here's how we take that seriously.

Garda vetting

Anyone doing relevant work with children on behalf of Get Debating — coaches, workshop facilitators, and volunteers who work directly with students — is Garda vetted in line with the National Vetting Bureau (Children and Vulnerable Persons) Acts 2012–2016, arranged through a registered relevant organisation. No one delivers a session unsupervised until their vetting is confirmed.

Children First & our Designated Liaison Person

We aim to operate in line with the Children First Act 2015 and the National Guidance for the Protection and Welfare of Children. Our Designated Liaison Person (DLP) is the named point of contact for any child protection concern raised in connection with our sessions.

How we work in schools

Sessions run with the knowledge and, where required, direct supervision of school staff. We follow each school's own child-protection procedures when we're on their premises, in addition to our own.

Photography & image use

We take a cautious, consent-first approach to any photo or video of students:

  • We seek written parental/guardian consent, routed through the school, before photographing or using any image of a student under 16. For 16–17-year-olds we seek the student's own consent and consider parental consent too.
  • Where possible, we favour photos taken from behind, over the shoulder, or in wide/anonymised shots that don't identify individual students, so we can show real sessions without relying on named consent for every image.
  • We use first names only, or no names, alongside any published photo — never a full name next to an image.
  • Consent can be withdrawn at any time; contact us and we'll remove the image.
  • Images are stored securely and are not kept on personal devices.
  • We do not use AI to generate or alter images of students.

Raising a concern

If you have a safeguarding concern about Get Debating's work, please contact our Designated Liaison Person directly, or email info@getdebating.ie marked "Safeguarding."
