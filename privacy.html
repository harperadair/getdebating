Trust & safety

Privacy policy.

This site is deliberately simple about data. Here's exactly what we collect and what we do with it.

What we collect

The only personal data this website collects is what you choose to give us through the enquiry form on our Contact page: your name, email address, school or organisation, role, and the content of your message. If you email us directly, we hold whatever you send in that email.

What we use it for

We only use your details to respond to your enquiry — to get back to you about workshops, coaching, events, or whatever you've asked about. We don't sell, rent, or share your details with third parties, and we don't use your details for marketing unless you've separately and explicitly asked to hear from us.

How it's stored

Enquiry form submissions are processed via Web3Forms, a third-party form-handling service, and delivered to a monitored Get Debating email inbox. We don't run a separate database or CRM. Enquiries are kept only as long as needed to respond and for a reasonable follow-up period, then deleted.

Cookies & analytics

This site does not use tracking cookies, marketing cookies, or third-party analytics. Because we don't set any non-essential cookies, we don't need to show a cookie-consent banner.

Children & the age of digital consent

The age of digital consent in Ireland is 16. Our enquiry form is intended to be completed by teachers, school staff, or parents/ guardians — not directly by students under 16. If a young person under 16 wants to get in touch, we ask that a parent, guardian, or teacher submits the enquiry on their behalf.

Your rights

Under GDPR, you can ask us what personal data we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. To do any of these, email info@getdebating.ie.

Questions

If anything here is unclear, or you have a question about how we handle your data, email info@getdebating.ie.
