About us

Built by debaters, for Dublin schools.

Get Debating is an organisation bringing workshops, coaching, and student debate groups to secondary schools across Dublin.

Our mission

Helping every student find a clear, confident voice

We think debating is one of the most useful things a student can learn — not just for competitions, but for school, work, and everyday life. Our mission is to make good debating coaching accessible to Dublin schools, whether or not they already have a debating tradition.

Who's behind it

Run by people who've lived the debating circuit

Founded by experienced members of Dublin's debating community, including former auditors of Trinity College Dublin's Hist and organisers of Leinster Schools Debating.

That background shapes how we teach: practical, competition-aware, and rooted in real experience of what helps a student improve — not just theory.

Governance

How we're set up

Not-for-profit, safeguarding-first

Get Debating is a not-for-profit voluntary organisation. We are not currently registered with the Charities Regulator — once we are, we'll display our registration number here. All coaches who work directly with students are Garda vetted, and we operate in line with Children First. Read our full safeguarding & child protection statement.
