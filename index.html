We help Dublin secondary school students with workshops, coaching and competition prep. We work directly with schools to build students' confidence, sharpen their arguments and teach productive disagreement.

Who's behind it

Founded by experienced members of Dublin's debating community. Tom and Jane both served as heads of Trinity College Dublin's College Historical Society and organised Leinster Schools Debating.

We work to bring debating to the classroom.

How we work

Three ways we work with students

Every school and group is different, so our work takes a few different shapes.

Strand 01

In-school workshops

Short, high-energy sessions that introduce argument structure, public speaking, and rebuttal.

Strand 02

Long-term debate coaching

A sustained programme across a term or year, with competition prep and regular feedback.

Strand 03

Private debate coaching

One-to-one coaching for students who want focused, personalised debate development outside the classroom.

Who it's for

Built for schools, teachers, parents, and students

Schools

Bring workshops or a full coaching programme into your school, or get support for an existing debating society.

Teachers

A ready-made programme you don't have to build from scratch, run by people who know the debating circuit.

Parents & students

A way to build public speaking and argument skills outside class, including for students not currently on a school team.
