Who's behind it

Founded by experienced members of Dublin's debating community, including former auditors of Trinity College Dublin's Hist and organisers of Leinster Schools Debating.

We work directly with schools, teachers, and students across Dublin — bringing that experience into the classroom.

Why it matters

What debating actually builds

Debating isn't just about winning an argument. It's a structured way to practise skills that carry into every part of school life.

Confidence & public speaking

Standing up, structuring a point, and speaking clearly under time pressure.

Critical thinking

Building an argument that holds together, and spotting where someone else's doesn't.

Listening & responding

Engaging with what's actually been said, not a rehearsed script.

Structured argument

Making a case in a clear order, so it's easy for a listener to follow.

Civic confidence

Getting comfortable discussing issues that matter, even when opinions differ.

Respectful disagreement

Disagreeing with an argument without it becoming a fight.
